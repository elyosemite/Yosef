## Overview

The **Project Management Service** is a .NET 9 C# microservice that owns the organizational hierarchy of the Yosef platform. It is the first service delivered and serves as the foundation upon which Quotation and Policy services operate.

**Responsibilities:**
- CRUD for Brokerages (formerly Organizations)
- Registration and management of Brokers under a Brokerage
- Registration of Insureds under a Broker
- Management of Projects (insurance product lines) owned by a Brokerage

**Architecture patterns used:** DDD, Clean Architecture, CQRS (MediatR), Outbox Pattern, Event Sourcing.
