## Overview

Emitted when an Insured is registered under a Broker. An Insured is the natural person or legal entity that will hold an insurance Policy.

**Business rule:** An Insured belongs to exactly one Broker. Contact fields (document, email, phone) are TBD — subject to the next grilling session on Insured ownership and identity.

{
  "insuredId": "e5e29b12-0b40-4c3b-9d28-9a3b5c0f0e1d",

  "name": "João da Silva",
