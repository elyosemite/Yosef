## Overview

The **Insurance Cycle** domain covers the full lifecycle of an insurance contract:

1. **Quotation** — a Broker generates a pricing proposal for an Insured on a given Project.
2. **Policy** — an approved Quotation becomes a binding Policy (the insurance contract).
3. **Claim** — an Insured files a claim against an active Policy when a loss event occurs.
