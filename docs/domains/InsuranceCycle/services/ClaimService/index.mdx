## Overview

The **Claim Service** is mission-critical — claims are the "moment of truth" for Insureds. It manages the complete lifecycle from First Notice of Loss (FNOL) to settlement.

**Actors:** Insureds (Policyholders), Agents/Brokers, Claims Adjusters, Back-office/Insurer Staff.

**Claim lifecycle:**
```
Registered → Under Investigation → Awaiting Documents → Approved/Rejected → Settled
```

**Technology:** Gleam / Wisp (Erlang BEAM). Chosen for strong type safety, fault-tolerance, and concurrency — critical for a claims engine handling complex workflows.

**Packages:** Squirrel (SQL), Pog (PostgreSQL), Wisp (HTTP API).

**Key features:**
- FNOL registration for clients or agents
- Integration with Policy Service for coverage validation
- Evidence & document handling
- Fraud detection hooks
- Settlement management via Payment Service
- Full audit trail for regulatory compliance
