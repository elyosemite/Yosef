## Overview

The **Product Service** allows insurance specialists and actuaries to create and publish insurance products without developer intervention. It powers the Quotation Service's pricing engine.

**Actors:** Product specialists, Actuaries, Brokers.

**Key features:**
- Product catalog management
- Dynamic form builder (multi-choice, numeric, boolean, conditional questions)
- Parametrization engine (weights, factors, pricing rules)
- Product versioning and publishing workflow
- Sandbox testing for pricing formulas
